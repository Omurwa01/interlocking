Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our interlocking bricks and services.

Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery services for all our interlocking brick products. Delivery fees depend on your location and the volume of your order. Please contact our sales team for a precise delivery quote.
What is the minimum order quantity?
Our minimum order quantity is 500 bricks for standard models. For custom orders or specialized bricks, please contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.
Do you provide installation services?
While we don't directly provide installation services, we have partnerships with trusted contractors who specialize in interlocking brick installations. We'd be happy to recommend professionals for your project.
Can I get samples before placing a large order?
Absolutely! We encourage clients to inspect our product quality. Sample kits are available at a nominal fee, which can be credited toward your full order if you proceed with a purchase.
